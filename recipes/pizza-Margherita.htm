Pizza Margherita | Odin Recipes

Preparation time: 15 min Cooking time: 7 min

Great pizza because it's simple

They say you can get a rough idea about the quality of a pizza place by ordering this simple yet excellent pizza.

Dough ingredients
  • 500 g wheat flour (high content of gluten)
  • 325 ml warm water
  • 7 g dry or 25 g fresh - brewer's yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 20 g wheat flour for kneading
  • enough semolina to cover the countertop
Tomatoes sauce
  • 1 can with 400 g of tomatoe pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove
  • salt, pepper
  • 3â€“5 leaves of fresh basil
Instead of tomatoes sauce you can use
  • 1 can of tomatoe paste + a little bit of salt and dried basil
Topping
  • 500 g mozzarella
  • 100 g of finely shredded parmesan (optional)
  • 1 tablespoons olive oil to splatter the pizza

Steps

  1. Dough

    2. The dough is to be made according to the recipe from Savori Urbane

  2. Tomatoes sauce

    3. Can be made according to the recipe from Savori Urbane

  3. Dough preparation and toppings

    4. Preheat the oven at 250 degrees Celsius, with the baking stone in it, laid on the lower most tray. The best results can be achieved heating the oven only on its bottom side with no air ventilation.

    Create the circular pizza shape, thinner in the middle and thicker around the margins. Spread the tomatoe sauce evenly, add a little bit of salt and dried basil. Cut mozzarella in triangular shapes, add them and top it up with olive oil.

    Slide the pizza in the oven at 250 deg. C, set the timer to 7 min. and wait. When ready, take the pizza out of the oven (Careful, it is hot!), add some fresh basil leaves. Once cooled enough, it can be sliced. Buon appetito!!
